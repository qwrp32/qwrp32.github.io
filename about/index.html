ABOUT US

PT. COSMAR

Who Are We?

PT. COSMAR is Indonesia leading cosmetics contract manufacturer. We develop and manufacture high quality cosmetics, with a focus on, face, body, hair and professional use formulations. Providing high level services to up and coming businesses, large established brands, dermatologists, and other skin & hair care professionals; we specialize in custom formulization and manufacturing of high quality cosmetics products.
Thanks to our experienced R&D team, in-house laboratory and extensive ingredient inventory, we ensure that our formulas meet your demands. We can create custom formulas or formula adaptations, or you can choose from our tried and tested large formula bank and color shades selection.
Take advantage of our ISO 22716 certified factory. Our facility is equipped for the creation of any number of new or revised product lines. A one stop organization based out of Tangerang, Indonesia, established in 2002 and with much local experience; we can help you in all stages of production, from concept to completion.
We have the experience and commitment to quality to exceed your expectations. We will partner with you to build your brand successfully.

Blue and green are the main colors of COSMAR. Theblue color reflects professional and trusted identity. Green color means constantly improving quality and high hygiene.

Our History

  • 2003

    PT. COSMAR was established and opened first factory at Pulo Gadung, Jakarta.

  • 2005

    Acquired ISO 9001, Quality Management Certification.

  • 2008

    Move to fully owned facilities in Taman Tekno BSD, Tangerang.

  • 2009

    First export to Middle East and South Asia.

  • 2014

    Acquired ISO 22716, Cosmetic GMP Guidline on Good Manufacturing Practices certification

  • 2017

    Acquired Halal Assurance System Certification by MUI

  • 2018

    Production facility expansion with total area of 4.200m and adding new machines fully automated filling lines with total annual capacities more that 50 millions unit.

As a succesful global leader in contract manufacturing of cosmetic products, PT. COSMAR implements management system with the best quality in practice and procedure.
The expansion of our production facilities continues to keep pace with the latest technological development and trends.
